What Does Our Patient Journey Framework (PJF) Do?

It contextualises the entire health economy and market, stakeholder influences, financial and resource constraints, competitive landscape, and behavioural science investigations of patients and HCPs all around a proprietary 12-step framework.

Insights should be developed in teams, and this unifying and optimised lens focused on the patient as the core (but far from only aspect) allows all team members to align around one common understanding of the patient journey—but also how different segments require different things throughout this journey.

Ultimately, all of this contextualised intelligence produces a series of strategic vectors or potential pivot points that form the basis and trigger for strategic choice development.

The 12-Step Framework

Our proprietary framework integrates health economics, market dynamics, competitive intelligence, stakeholder mapping, and behavioural science into a cohesive strategic tool. This comprehensive approach ensures no critical factor is overlooked, creating a complete picture of the healthcare landscape.

Team-Based Insight Development

Strategic insights are most powerful when developed collaboratively. Our approach brings cross-functional teams together around a shared understanding, ensuring that diverse perspectives contribute to richer, more robust strategic outcomes while maintaining alignment on core objectives.

Patient-Centric, But Holistic

While the patient remains at the center of our framework, we recognize that effective healthcare strategy must account for the entire ecosystem. Our approach balances patient needs with stakeholder interests, market realities, and resource constraints to create strategies that are both ideal and achievable.
